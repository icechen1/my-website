About me

Hi! I am a first year Software Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I consider technology and programming one of my greatest passions, as I contantly learn new things and solve problems while working on projects.
I am fascinated by how something as small as an app can reach and improve the life of many and I strive to work on projects that can impact people.

I am familiar with Java, Python, C#, C, web design, SQL, Javascript (Angularjs and Nodejs) and PHP. As a hobby, I develop various Android web apps, some of which are available on Google Play.
On my free time I also enjoy photography, geocaching, biking, hiking, participating in hackathons, and occasionally playing games.
To reach me, simply shoot me an e-mail! I'd love to discuss with you any project or work you may have in mind.
